About the model.

Backbone
efficientnet-b3 — a 12M-parameter CNN pretrained on ImageNet, used as a shared encoder for both the classification and segmentation heads. Encoder weights are fine-tuned at a 10× lower learning rate than the task heads.
Classification
Global average pooling on the deepest encoder feature map, followed by a two-layer MLP (Dropout → Linear(256) → GELU → Dropout → Linear(3)) projecting to three logits: 1st / 2nd / 3rd degree.
Segmentation
U-Net decoder with skip connections from the EfficientNet-B3 encoder stages. Produces a binary mask (wound vs. not-wound) at 384×384, bilinearly upsampled to the original image resolution. Sigmoid activation — no explicit background class.
Training
60 epochs, AdamW with warmup-cosine LR schedule, gradient accumulation (effective batch 32), class-weighted focal loss for classification, combined BCE + Dice loss for segmentation, EMA (decay 0.998), mixed precision on a single T4.
Inference
4-way test-time augmentation (identity, horizontal flip, vertical flip, both) with softmax/sigmoid averaging over the four views. Final weights are taken from the EMA shadow model.
Limitations
Trained on a limited dataset of curated images. The segmentation mask is binary (wound vs. not-wound) — per-class area percentages in the results are derived from the classification prediction, not independent per-class masks.
